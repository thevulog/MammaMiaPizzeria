From Napoli to Perth, Bringing the Heart of Neapolitan Pizza to Your Table!

Mamma Mia Pizzeria began as a dream born in the cobblestone streets of Napoli, where Vu Nguyen, an ambitious chef with a passion for authentic flavors, spent a decade mastering the art of Neapolitan pizza. Under the mentorship of local pizza masters, Vu perfected every detailâ€”from hand-stretching dough to layering the freshest ingredients in true Italian style. After ten years in Italy, Vu returned to Perth, eager to bring this slice of Naples to his home city. At Mamma Mia Pizzeria, each pizza is crafted with tradition, passion, and a touch of Italian soul.

As Mamma Mia Pizzeria expands to four locations across Perth, Executive Chef Vu Nguyen remains deeply rooted in the belief that pizza should be simple and crafted with passion, reflecting the rich heritage of Neapolitan flavors while celebrating the local produce and community values of Perth. After ten years of immersing himself in the art of pizza-making in Napoli, Vu brings a fresh approach to Italian classics, fusing time-honored recipes with the finest local ingredients. By balancing tradition with innovation, Mamma Mia Pizzeria invites guests to experience a taste of Napoli, thoughtfully reimagined for the Perth community.




And so, a new chapter in Perth's food scene was born!
