Parbaked Neopolitan Frozen Pizzas

Where to buy

Enjoy our par-baked pizzas at home! Available at select grocery stores.

Our frozen pizzas are available at various supermarket an indepedent grocery stores around the Perth Metropolitan area.

Reheating Instructions

Follow these simple steps to enjoy your Mamma Mia Pizzeria pizza at home:

Step 1

Preheat your oven to 220Â°C. Make sure it's fully heated before adding the pizza.

Step 2

Place the frozen pizza directly on the middle oven rack for the best crispiness.

Step 3

Bake for 10-12 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and cheese is bubbly. Enjoy!
